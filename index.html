Expense Calculator

An Expense Calculator is a simple and useful tool that helps users manage and divide financial costs efficiently. It allows you to input the total amount of money spent and the number of people sharing the expense. With just a click, it quickly calculates how much each person needs to pay. This kind of calculator is especially helpful in situations like splitting bills during trips, group meals, or shared living expenses. By automating the math, it reduces confusion, saves time, and ensures fair cost distribution among all participants.
